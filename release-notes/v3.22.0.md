## What this release is — and what it deliberately is not

v3.22 is the fleet's **first deliberately-ambitious minor cycle** (principal directive, 2026-06-12): scope was locked **over** the conservative `RUBRIC_minor_release_scope` band by accepted design, run as an **instrumented experiment** (hypothesis H-AMB-322). The point was not to over-commit — it was to *measure the real ambitious-cycle capacity* against a baseline rubric calibrated on N=1 and self-documented as "conservative by 3–4×."

It is **not** a feature-maximalist release. The real deliverable target was **T1+T2 (14 items)**; the 12 Tier-3 items were explicit stretch, expected to slip — and their slip is calibration data, not failure.
